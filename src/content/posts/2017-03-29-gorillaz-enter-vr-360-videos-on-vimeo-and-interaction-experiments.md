#### [Experiment: Accurate Modelling in VR](https://virtualrealitypop.com/experiment-accurate-modelling-in-vr-b16cb0395e2b#.bzr07m8tz)

Interaction Designer Eyal Shahar has been doing some experimentation with VR. In this post (and accompanying video), he shares some of his findings on interactive free form modeling with minimal UI.

#### [Vimeo Adds Support for 360 Videos](https://join.vimeo.com/360/)

I’ve been wondering when Vimeo would make the leap. They’ve also added a whole section to their blog on the fundamentals of creating 360 videos. You can check that out [here](https://vimeo.com/blog/category/360).

#### [Gorillaz — Saturnz Barz (Spirit House)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lVaBvyzuypw) (YouTube — Free)

The Gorillaz join the ranks of artists putting out 360 videos! Check out their latest single on YouTube. It starts a little slow by forcing you to be in a 360 space while watching a 2d screen, but it quickly opens up.
